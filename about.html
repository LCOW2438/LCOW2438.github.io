Our History

Founded in 2010, Switch On started as a small boutique lamp store in downtown. Our mission was to illuminate lives with customized lighting solutions that reflect our customers' unique styles and needs.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable lighting solutions that enhance the way people live and work. We are committed to excellence in design, quality, and customer service, striving to be the leading source for lighting products and expertise.
